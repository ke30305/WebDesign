About Me

I’m a UIUX designer with a passion for solving problems and turning ideas into products people love. I’ve over 7 years in various design fields - Including digital learning, financial system, and E-commerce industry.
I worked as a visual/web designer for past 4 years. But I realized there was something missing when I worked in Ihergo team as a UI designer, so I decided to change gears - learning about UIUX design process and knowledges. It helps me to figure out the purpose in design, and taken my creativity and potential to a whole new level. I believed that good design should not only look great, but also be usable and truly let people reach the goal.
